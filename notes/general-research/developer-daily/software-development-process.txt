1. Analysis and planning
- Alignment:  Does this project connect to your company's larger mission and goals?
- Resource availability and alocation: Do you have the people and tools you need to take this on? Or do you need to hire a new development team? 
- Project scheduling: How does this project fit within your company's goals and other tasks?
- Cost scheduling: How does this project fit within your company's goals and other tasks? 
- Cost estimation: How much is it going to cost?
